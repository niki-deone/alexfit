My story

Coaching that goes beyond the gym

I started training at 19 after years of feeling lost in the gym — following random programs from the internet and seeing no results. I decided to learn everything properly, got certified, and never stopped studying.

Eight years later I've worked with over 340 clients: office workers, athletes, new moms, people recovering from injuries. What I've learned is that the best program is the one that fits your life, not the other way around.

I'm based in Berlin Mitte but work with clients across Europe online. Whether you want to lose 20kg or add 20kg to your deadlift, I'll build a program that gets you there.
